Contact FAQ

What information should I send for a quotation?

Please share the product category, drawings or samples, material, estimated quantity, thread or interface details, surface treatment, application and inspection or packaging requirements where available.

Can I send drawings or 3D models by email?

Yes. You can send drawings, 3D models and technical files by email to kisaweng@outlook.com for project review.

Can you manufacture parts from physical samples?

Sample-based development can be discussed after the physical samples and application requirements are evaluated.

Which product categories can I inquire about?

You can inquire about hydraulic fittings, adapters, welded tube assemblies, manifold blocks, cylinder parts, valve bodies, valve spools, pistons and custom CNC parts.

Can custom materials, threads and finishes be discussed?

Yes. Materials, threads, interface standards and surface treatments can be discussed and confirmed according to the project requirements.

How can I arrange a factory visit?

Please contact us in advance by email, phone or WhatsApp so a factory visit can be arranged.
