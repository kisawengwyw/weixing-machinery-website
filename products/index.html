How to Choose Hydraulic Components

  • Confirm the thread standard or connection type, such as ORFS, BSP, JIC or NPT.
  • Confirm the sealing method required by the hydraulic circuit and working conditions.
  • Confirm material and surface treatment based on corrosion resistance, strength and appearance needs.
  • Confirm working pressure, temperature, vibration and application environment.
  • Send drawings or samples when custom CNC parts or non-standard components are required.

Products FAQ

What hydraulic products does Wei Xing Machinery supply?

We supply hydraulic fittings, adapters, welded tube assemblies, manifold blocks, valve bodies, valve spools, cylinder parts and custom CNC machined components.

Can products be customized by drawing?

Yes. We can manufacture non-standard parts according to customer drawings, specifications and technical requirements.

Which thread standards are available?

Common options include ORFS, BSP, JIC and NPT connections, with custom interfaces reviewed according to drawings or samples.

What materials are available?

Available materials include carbon steel, stainless steel, alloy steel, aluminum and brass, selected according to pressure, corrosion and machining requirements.

What surface treatments are available?

Surface treatment options include trivalent zinc plating, zinc nickel, black oxide, phosphating, passivation and polishing.

How do I choose between fittings, manifolds and valve components?

Choose fittings for line connections, manifold blocks for integrated hydraulic circuits, and valve components for flow control or motion-control assemblies.

Can samples be used for quotation?

Yes. Samples can help confirm dimensions, structure, material and surface treatment when drawings are not complete.

How do I request a quote?

Send drawings, samples, material requirements, quantities and application information through the contact page or WhatsApp.

Can't Find What You Need?

Send us your drawings or specifications. Custom manufacturing can be discussed according to drawings, samples, quantities and project requirements.
