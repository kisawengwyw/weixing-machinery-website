Hydraulic Cylinder Components

Hydraulic Cylinder Parts

Hydraulic cylinder parts include pistons, glands, rod ends, clevises, bushings, welding heads, ports and other components used in hydraulic cylinders. These parts must withstand pressure, sliding movement, mechanical load and long-term wear. Accurate machining, suitable material selection and proper surface treatment are important for cylinder sealing, guiding and connection performance. Hydraulic cylinder parts are used in construction machinery, lifting equipment, agricultural machines, industrial presses and custom hydraulic actuator systems.

Selection Advice

Hydraulic Cylinder Parts

  • Choose hydraulic cylinder parts according to cylinder bore, rod diameter, pressure rating and movement load.
  • For sealing components, confirm groove dimensions, surface roughness and seal material before production.
  • Use alloy steel or heat-treated parts for high-load or fatigue-sensitive cylinder applications.
  • For replacement parts, provide the original sample or detailed drawing to avoid assembly mismatch.
  • Select corrosion-resistant surface treatment when cylinders are used outdoors, in marine areas or in wet environments.
  • For batch production, define inspection points clearly, including sealing grooves, threads and concentricity.
