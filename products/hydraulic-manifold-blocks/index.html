Hydraulic Manifold Blocks

Hydraulic manifold blocks are precision-machined metal blocks used to distribute, control and connect hydraulic oil flow inside compact hydraulic systems. They integrate multiple ports, threaded cavities, valve mounting surfaces and internal flow passages into one component, reducing external piping and improving system organization. Manifold blocks can be manufactured from aluminum, carbon steel or stainless steel according to pressure and application needs. They are used in power units, cartridge valve systems, mobile machinery and industrial hydraulic equipment.

Selection Advice

Hydraulic Manifold Blocks

  • Choose hydraulic manifold blocks when the system needs compact oil distribution, fewer external pipes and cleaner hydraulic control.
  • Provide a hydraulic schematic before production so internal passages and valve functions can be designed correctly.
  • Select aluminum for lightweight and moderate-pressure designs, and steel for heavy-duty or high-pressure circuits.
  • Confirm port standards early to avoid mismatching BSP, NPT, SAE, metric, JIC or ORFS fittings during assembly.
  • Request internal cleaning and pressure testing for complex blocks used in sensitive hydraulic equipment.
  • For repeat production, lock the drawing version and inspection standard to maintain stable quality.
