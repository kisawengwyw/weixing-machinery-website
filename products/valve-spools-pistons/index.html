Valve Spools & Pistons

Product Summary

Valve spools and pistons are high-precision cylindrical hydraulic components requiring controlled grooves, lands, radial holes, seal grooves, cylindricity and surface roughness. They are manufactured for close-fit movement, sealing performance and repeatable hydraulic control.

Valve spools and pistons are precision cylindrical components used in hydraulic valves, control valves, cylinders and servo-related mechanisms. They control fluid direction, pressure, flow or mechanical movement by sliding inside matched bores, sleeves or cylinder bodies. These parts require accurate diameter tolerance, smooth surface finish, groove geometry and wear-resistant material. Valve spools and pistons are commonly produced by CNC turning, grinding, polishing and heat treatment for stable hydraulic control and long service life.

Selection Advice

Valve Spools & Pistons Selection Advice

1. Confirm drawing requirements. Provide key dimensions, tolerances, threads, holes, grooves and surface roughness.

2. Confirm material. Select material according to pressure, weldability, corrosion resistance and cost.

3. Confirm surface finish. Specify salt-spray, color, coating or anti-rust packaging requirements.

4. Confirm inspection focus. Mark sealing faces, fitted bores, threads and critical dimensions.
