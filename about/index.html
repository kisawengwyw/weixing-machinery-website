About Us

Who We Are

Yuyao Wei Xing Machinery Co., Ltd. is a precision manufacturer of hydraulic fittings, hydraulic adapters, welded tube assemblies, hydraulic manifold blocks, valve components, hydraulic cylinder parts and custom CNC machined components. We support hydraulic equipment manufacturers, industrial machinery companies and OEM customers with drawing-based and sample-based production.

With stable manufacturing capability, strict quality control, and flexible customization services, we provide reliable hydraulic connection solutions for customers in construction machinery, agricultural equipment, automotive, marine, energy, and industrial applications. Over the years, our products have served companies across East Asia, Europe, and North America, earning long-term trust from global customers.

We are committed to quality, customer-focused service, and delivering high-precision, stable, and cost-effective hydraulic fitting products worldwide.

What We Manufacture

Main Product Range

Hydraulic Fittings

Precision fittings for hydraulic hose and pipeline connections, manufactured to support reliable sealing and assembly in industrial equipment.

BSP Hydraulic Adapters

Threaded adapters and transition connectors for matching hydraulic ports, tubes and hoses across different interface requirements.

Welded Tube Assemblies

Custom welded tube and pipe assemblies made according to drawings, routing dimensions and application requirements.

Hydraulic Manifold Blocks

Machined manifold blocks with ports, cavities and oil passages arranged to customer hydraulic circuit drawings.

Hydraulic Valve Bodies

Valve bodies and related housings for hydraulic valve component machining, with controlled sealing surfaces, ports and internal features.

Valve Spools & Pistons

Spools, pistons and precision turned components for hydraulic control and cylinder applications requiring stable dimensions.

Hydraulic Cylinder Parts

Cylinder-related components such as glands, end caps, bushings and machined parts made to drawing specifications.

Custom CNC Parts

Non-standard CNC turned and milled components for machinery, hydraulic systems and OEM assembly projects.

Custom Manufacturing Support

We support OEM manufacturing based on 2D drawings, 3D models, physical samples, thread and interface requirements, material selection and surface treatment requirements. Small-batch trial production and repeat production can be arranged according to project needs.

Materials & Processes

Common materials include carbon steel, stainless steel, alloy steel, aluminum and brass. Manufacturing and coordinated processes include CNC turning, CNC milling, drilling, tapping, boring, thread machining, welding coordination and surface treatment coordination.

Surface treatment options can be arranged according to drawing and project requirements, including commonly used industrial finishes such as zinc plating, zinc nickel, black oxide, phosphating, passivation and polishing.

FAQ

About Wei Xing Machinery

What does Wei Xing Machinery manufacture?

Wei Xing Machinery manufactures hydraulic fittings, hydraulic adapters, welded tube assemblies, hydraulic manifold blocks, hydraulic valve bodies, valve spools, pistons, hydraulic cylinder parts and custom CNC machined components.

Can parts be manufactured from drawings?

Yes. We support drawing-based manufacturing using customer 2D drawings, 3D models and technical specifications.

Can physical samples be used for development?

Yes. Physical samples can be reviewed to confirm dimensions, threads, materials, surface finish and application requirements before production planning.

Which materials can be machined?

Commonly machined materials include carbon steel, stainless steel, alloy steel, aluminum and brass, subject to drawing and project requirements.

Do you support custom threads and interfaces?

Yes. We can manufacture parts with custom threads and hydraulic interfaces according to drawings, samples or confirmed application requirements.

What information is required before production?

Before production, please provide drawings or samples, material requirements, thread and interface details, surface treatment needs, quantity, application notes and inspection requirements if available.
