About Us

Who We Are

We are a professional hydraulic fittings manufacturer based in China, with more than 20 years of industry experience. We specialize in the development and production of hydraulic fittings, adapters, hose fittings, welded tube assemblies, valve bodies, and custom CNC machined components.

With stable manufacturing capability, strict quality control, and flexible customization services, we provide reliable hydraulic connection solutions for customers in construction machinery, agricultural equipment, automotive, marine, energy, and industrial applications. Over the years, our products have served companies across East Asia, Europe, and North America, earning long-term trust from global customers.

We are committed to quality, customer-focused service, and delivering high-precision, stable, and cost-effective hydraulic fitting products worldwide.
